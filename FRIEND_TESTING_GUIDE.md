## Testing Tasks

### Task 1: First Impressions (5 minutes)

1. **Visit the homepage**: [YOUR_URL_HERE]
2. **Don't click anything yet!** Just look at the page for 30 seconds
3. **Note down**:
   - What's your first impression?
   - Is it clear what Foreignteer does?
   - Does anything confuse you?
   - Do you feel compelled to explore more?

### Task 2: Browse Experiences (10 minutes)

1. Click "Browse Experiences" or "Explore" in the navigation
2. Look at the available volunteering opportunities
3. Try the **filters**:
   - Search by location
   - Filter by category
   - Try the "Instant Confirmation" filter
4. Click on an experience to see details

**Questions to answer:**
- Are the experiences appealing?
- Is the information clear?
- Can you easily find what you're looking for?
- Do the filters work as expected?

### Task 3: Create an Account (5 minutes)

1. Click "Sign Up" in the top right
2. Create a **volunteer account** (not NGO)
   - Use your real email (you'll receive a test confirmation email)
   - Create a password
   - Check/uncheck the newsletter subscription as you prefer
3. Complete your profile

**Questions to answer:**
- Was signup easy?
- Did you receive a confirmation email?
- Any confusing fields?

### Task 4: Book an Experience (10 minutes)

1. Browse experiences again (now logged in)
2. Choose one that interests you
3. Click "Book Now" or "Apply Now"
4. Fill out the booking form
5. Submit your application

**What to check:**
- Does the booking flow make sense?
- Are the questions appropriate?
- Do you understand the difference between "Instant Confirmation" and "Requires Approval" experiences?
- Did you receive a booking confirmation email?

**Note**: If an experience requires approval, you won't be instantly confirmed. This is intentional!

### Task 5: Explore Your Dashboard (5 minutes)

1. After booking, click your profile icon ‚Üí "Dashboard"
2. Explore:
   - Your bookings
   - Profile settings
   - Any other features you see

**Questions to answer:**
- Can you easily find your booking?
- Is your dashboard organized well?
- Can you understand your booking status?

### Task 6: Read the Blog (5 minutes)

1. Navigate to the Blog section
2. Read one or two blog posts
3. Try the search feature
4. Try sharing a post on social media

**Questions to answer:**
- Are the blog posts interesting?
- Does search work well?
- Do share buttons work?

### Task 7: Newsletter Signup (2 minutes)

1. Scroll to the footer
2. Try signing up for the newsletter with a different email
3. Check if you receive a confirmation

**Questions to answer:**
- Is the newsletter box visible?
- Does signup work smoothly?

### Task 8: Explore Additional Pages (10 minutes)

Visit and review:
- [ ] About Us
- [ ] How It Works
- [ ] Partner With Us
- [ ] FAQ
- [ ] Contact Us

**Questions to answer:**
- Is the information helpful?
- Any broken links or typos?
- Anything missing that you'd want to know?

### Task 9: Mobile Testing (10 minutes) - OPTIONAL

If you have time, repeat key tasks on your phone:
- Browse experiences
- View experience details
- Check if everything looks good on mobile

**Questions to answer:**
- Does it work well on mobile?
- Any layout issues?
- Easy to navigate?

## How to Report Issues

### For Each Issue, Note:
1. **What happened**: Describe the problem
2. **Where**: Which page? (copy the URL)
3. **Steps to reproduce**: What did you do before the error?
4. **Screenshot**: Take a screenshot if possible
5. **Device**: Computer/mobile, browser (Chrome, Safari, etc.)

### Examples:

**Good Bug Report:**
```
Issue: Can't submit booking form
Page: /experiences/sea-turtle-conservation
Steps:
1. Clicked "Book Now"
2. Filled out all fields
3. Clicked "Submit"
4. Nothing happened, no error message
Browser: Chrome on Mac
Screenshot: [attached]
```

**Good Feedback:**
```
Suggestion: Add a "Favorites" feature
Why: I'd love to save experiences I'm interested in for later
Without scrolling through all experiences again
```
